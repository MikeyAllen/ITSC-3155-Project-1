A Little about Us!

We are a small school run buisness that sells plants grows and produced by students.
We of course offer potting suplies as well as other planting needs to help any anyone
no matter if they are just starting their planting journey or if they are regulars at
plant stores. All profits go back to more plants as well as scolarships for students.

Our Goal

Our goal here at the Greenhouse and Gardens at the University of North Carolina at
Charlotte is to help students conect with nature while at the same time helping
paying their bills and tuition. Studies show that even just 20 minutes a week in
nature without teachnology will improve the a persons mental health and alievate stress.
